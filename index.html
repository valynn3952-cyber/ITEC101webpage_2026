My Core Values

Values Industry
  • Integrity

    1. I value honesty and generaly strong morals in a person.

    2. As a kid in school they would repeat this phrase all the time, do the right thing even when no one is watching; and this value stuck with me.

    3. This is important because I want to act the way I would want someone else to act, I would want to be able to trust in people.

    4. Without this value people couldn't trust me, and a relationship without trust does not work.

  • Creativity

    1. Creativity is what keeps life interesting, and without it the world would just be bland.

    2. As a kid I was always very creative, I drew and I wrote stories. And those traits have stayed with me.

    3. It is important because creativity leads to new innovation and brings perspective to new things.

    4. Without creativity the world just would be as interesting, and would have new and surpising inventions or discoveries.

  • Growth

    1. It is important to always try and work on yourself, improve and grow.

    2. As an adolescence, I grew up with a parent who thought they were perfect the way they were, so made no effort to grow or work on themselves or their relationship. So their relationship dwindled away to resentment.

    3. Growth means working to be a better person, whether for yourself or for someone else, so it is so important to grow with time and not settle with good enough.

    4. If you do not grow, you will lose relationships and possible opportunities in life.

  • Kindness

    1. I value being kind to people especially people you don't know because you never know what someone is going through.

    2. When I was young all I had was kindness to get me through difficult situations, so I learned the importances fast.

    3. It is important because people are going through all kinds of experiences and treating someone kindly is a way to help just a little.

    4. And if you are not kind people tend to not be kind back, and people wont want to be around you.
