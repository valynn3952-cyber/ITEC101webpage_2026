  • Therapy

    The career that intrests me in healthcare is therapy. I want to talk one on one with a client and help them through their mental health. This is because I have always been intrested in psychology in general, and with this career I get to use this knowledge to help people.

  • Ethical Issue

    An important ethical issue is a clients confidentiality. Therapists are supposed to keep what clients say private. However, if a client claim they may hurt themselves or someone else, the therapist may have to share that information for the safety of the client and others.

  • Personal Values

    My values will help me with this career. I will show kindness and compassion towards a client allowing them to open up to me. With integrity the client will trust me with their private information, knowing I will keep it confidential. And using creativity I can help find a treatment plan to help them grow.

  • Cybersecurity Issues

    Some cybersecurity problems in this field include: Hackers stealing private client information, unauthorized people accessing records, and unsafe online therapy sessions.

  • Cybersecurity Best Practices

    To prevent these common problems, therapists should: Use secure encrypted systems, make strong passwords, keep software updated, only allow authorized people to see client information, and avoid scams like phishing.
